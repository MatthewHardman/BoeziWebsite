About Boezi

The story behind our approach to product development

John's Journey

John Boezi has spent over two decades developing products that matter. Starting with a passion for solving real-world problems, John has evolved from hands-on maker to strategic product developer, working with some of the world's most innovative companies and individuals.

His experience spans from initial concept through manufacturing at scale, giving him a unique perspective on what it takes to bring a product from idea to successful market launch. John's philosophy is simple: understand the user deeply, design with integrity, and never compromise on quality.

When not developing products, John can be found in the mountains, on the water, or in his workshop—staying connected to the adventurers, athletes, and builders who inspire his work.

Our Proven Process

We've refined our approach through hundreds of successful projects. This methodology ensures nothing falls through the cracks and every product reaches the market ready to succeed.

1. Discovery & Validation

We start by truly understanding your idea, market, and audience. We validate the concept, identify potential challenges early, and define clear success metrics. This stage is crucial—getting it right now saves time and money later.

Deliverables: Market research report, competitive analysis, product requirements document

2. Concept Development

We explore multiple design directions, evaluate feasibility, and narrow to the strongest concept. This involves sketching, 3D modeling, basic prototyping, and user feedback sessions.

Deliverables: Concept CAD models, design rationale, feasibility assessment

3. Detailed Design

Now we engineer the product to production quality. We handle material selection, structural optimization, manufacturability analysis, and prepare for tooling. Every detail is considered.

Deliverables: Production CAD files, engineering drawings, manufacturing specifications

4. Prototype & Testing

We build functional prototypes and subject them to rigorous testing. Real-world testing identifies issues before production, and user feedback drives refinements. We iterate until performance is validated.

Deliverables: Functional prototypes, test reports, certification documentation

5. Manufacturing Preparation

We select manufacturing partners, design tooling, establish quality processes, and run pilot production runs. We optimize for cost, quality, and timeline. Everything is tested before full production launch.

Deliverables: Manufacturing partner contracts, tooling designs, QC procedures, pilot run results

6. Market Launch

We support the launch phase, ensuring smooth production scaling, managing supply chain issues, and addressing any field issues quickly. Your product gets to market ready and your customers are satisfied.

Deliverables: Production monitoring, supply chain management, post-launch support
